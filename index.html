About

As a Character Artist, I am proficient and I've had experience as a Grooming, CFX (Strand and Cloth Simulation), Look-Dev, Texturing, Modelling and Sculpting: on both realistic and stylized projects.

What sets my work a part is my unique blend of technical and artistic vision. An intersection between realism and stylization. With a background in both films and games industries, I bring a flexible and deep understanding of different production pipelines. I am also passionate about collaboration, communication, learning and mentoring; having, not only learned, but also supported and taught fellow artists along the way.

In addition to my proffesional work, I love photography and personal projects that push my creativity and story telling further. This side is a space to share both my professional journey and personal passions.
